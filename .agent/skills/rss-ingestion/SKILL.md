name: rss-ingestion
description: Engineers and optimizes the automated RSS data ingestion pipeline, ensuring robust, high-throughput, and fault-tolerant aggregation of external news sources.

skill_overview: >
  This tool systematically audits the backend architecture responsible for continuous content aggregation. It focuses on system stability, data integrity, and the scalable processing of multiple concurrent feeds to maintain an up-to-date news repository.

pipeline_optimization_focus:
  - Robust Feed Parsing & Data Normalization (XML/Atom)
  - Advanced Deduplication Algorithms & Data Integrity
  - Resilient Error Handling & Exponential Backoff Retry Mechanisms
  - Feed Health Monitoring & Fallback Strategies
  - Throughput Optimization & Asynchronous Processing
  - Queue Management & Worker Scalability (e.g., leveraging Redis)

execution_directive: >
  Evaluate the complete ingestion lifecycle from the initial network request to the final database insertion. Identify processing bottlenecks and propose architectural enhancements to guarantee a scalable, zero-data-loss pipeline capable of handling high-frequency updates.
